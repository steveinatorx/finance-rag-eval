Financial Report Q1 2024

Company Overview
Our company reported strong financial performance in the first quarter of 2024. Total revenue reached $125 million, representing a 15% increase compared to the same period last year. This growth was driven primarily by increased sales in our technology division.

Revenue Breakdown
- Technology Division: $85 million (68% of total revenue)
- Services Division: $30 million (24% of total revenue)
- Consulting Division: $10 million (8% of total revenue)

Operating Expenses
Total operating expenses for the quarter were $95 million, including:
- Research and Development: $25 million
- Sales and Marketing: $30 million
- General and Administrative: $20 million
- Cost of Goods Sold: $20 million

Net Income
Net income for Q1 2024 was $30 million, with an operating margin of 24%. This represents a significant improvement from the previous quarter's margin of 18%.

Future Outlook
We expect continued growth in the technology division, with projected revenue of $90 million for Q2 2024. The company is investing heavily in research and development to maintain its competitive advantage.
